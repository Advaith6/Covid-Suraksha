Covid - Suraksha

Let's Fight Covid Together!!!

My main aim by creating this app is to help people know all about Corona, its symptoms, precautions, facts, and defend against it. My app has more than 60 screens and awesome UI/UX design, making it very easy to use and navigate. I have built this app on a platform called kodular creator which is like thunkable but easier to use. My app contains many advanced features like sign-in and sign-up pages, a splash screen, animations, and much more. It also contains more than five special features, one of them is the donate feature where you can donate money, donate food or donate your old clothes to someone you know or the top five orphanages that are shown in the app by just entering the recipient's UPI id. One other cool feature in the app is the emergency button feature, if you are in an emergency, you can just click on the emergency button which makes an immediate call to the Indian private ambulance service and send a text message to the ambulance. With this, I conclude my description of my Covid - Suraksha App.
